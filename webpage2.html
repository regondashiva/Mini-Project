Dhyana yoga -  The Science of Self Realization
This chapter provides practical methods of meditation for one who has purified the mind by diligently engaging in karma-yoga described in the previous chapters. For such a seeker, a life of contemplation is suggested along with an assurance that the path of meditation inevitably leads to the ultimate goal of Realization whether it be in the current life or a future one.
